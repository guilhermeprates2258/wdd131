Paraná, Brazil

Paraná is a state located in the south of Brazil, bordered by São Paulo, Santa Catarina, and the Atlantic Ocean. Its capital is Curitiba, known for innovation and quality of life. The state is famous for its agriculture, with highlights including grain, meat, and dairy production. Paraná is also rich in biodiversity, with Iguaçu National Park, home to the Iguaçu Falls, one of the country's major tourist attractions. The economy is diverse, including industries and services.
